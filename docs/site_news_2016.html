Change of temporary code name : Limacella sp-L-WA02 is the new name for Limacella sp-Krstic-20-xi-2010.  We have decided to slowly move to increased use of geographically-based code names...especially when depositing sequences in GenBank.

Thanks to users who have helped find broken links on the North American Pacific Coast checklist.  It looks like some of them have been there awhile.  We ask for continued help with problems on this site and are very grateful for corrections.

We are also grateful for corrections of facts and typos on species pages.  RET is prone to some typos, and we're always glad when anyone catches one.

Every once in awhile someone asks where to find photographers' credits on the site.  Every taxon page has an "images" tab.  Credits for all images appearing on a given taxon page will be found on the "images" tab for that page.  This format has been part of the site's design from the outset.  A new brief "teaching topic" has been added providing this information on all taxon pages.

Removal of taxon page: The taxon page for Amanita sp-N17 has been removed and its content has been merged with A. porphyria.

The name "stirps Mappa" has been replaced by Singer's original "stirps Citrina" to reduce confusion and provide historic continuity.  Singer's usage originated in the third and fourth editions of his "Agaricales" (Singer 1975, 1986).

Simplified characterizations were provided for stirpes Citrina and Brunnescens and series Mappae.
