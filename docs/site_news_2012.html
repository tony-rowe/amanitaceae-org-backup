December 19 2012:

Slow response time: For the last three days users of the site have experienced degraded response time for page loads.  The problem has been traced to a site to which we interface for some services that are rarely if ever used by our visitors. We terminated the connections to that site, and our response time appears to be reasonable again.

December 15 2012:

Site usage: Today this site received its 43,000th visit of 2012 and has now delivered to its users 22.38 GBytes of data in the present year.

New site feature: Yesterday, we posted a searchable, sortable, filterable version of our previously private DNA sequence collection catalog.  We did this not because we thought the page is in finished condition. (There is more that we want to do with it.) We made it public because we have the expectation that the number of sequences available on the site is going to greatly increase in size over the coming months, and we wanted our users to be able to look through the data for their own purposes as soon as the data is available.

The page in question is www.amanitaceae.org?Amanitaceae+genetic+sequence+collection.

Deleted page: Limacella oaxacana is excluded on the grounds that its apparently sterile gill margin, gelatinized "hymenopodium," and partially gelatinized lamella trama are incompatible with inclusion in the genus Limacella.  The spores are also rather large for a species of Limacella.  Nevertheless, we hope to be able to review the original material.
