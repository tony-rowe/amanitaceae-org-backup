13 August 2023:

Added directory page: All links to color variants of Amanita chrysoblema are consolidated on a single page which is named "species chrysoblema."

7 August 2023:

page changes: Amanita sp-T53 is merged with A. trygonion and the temporary code is abandoned permanently.  This means sp-T35 disappears from the provisional subsection Penetratrices.

Updated techtabs: Amanita penetratrix.

4-6 August 2023:

Updated techtabs: Extensive additions were made to Amanita penetratrix in the discussion of subsection Penetratrices.  Both sources for membership in the Penetratrices are extended and complete to the extent of our knowledge. We are targetting production of an updated morphological characterization of the subsection with the hope of clearly excluding A. petersenii on morphological grounds.  We also are targeting enough phylogenetic work to find "natural" subdivisions of the subsection.

23 July 2023:

New taxon page: A new provisional species of section Vaginatae is added to the site from North Carolina—A. taciturna. It is unassuming or "quiet" because it lacks bright colors and doesn't call attention to itself.&mbsp; However, its ITS-LSU sequences are very distinctive.  A skeleton page has been posted.

23 July 2023: The range of Amanita albiceps is extended from Quebec to Wisconsin and Texas.

A number of new sequences (ITS, ITS-LSU, and LSU) have been posted to GenBank and GB accession numbers are being posted to the WAO website today.

19 January 2023:

All herbarium shelves are accessible. There is still lots of junk piled in the herbarium, but the room is about to be fully usable in a post flood form.  That is to say, everything that should be there is there, and everything that should be somewhere else is still there in part.  About 24 hours away is a workable work table and a workable small desk...cross fingers.

We're working on submission of many sequences to GenBank, with particular focus on section Amanita section Amidella.  The genes targeted are nrITS, nrLSU, and rpb2.
